PLACEMENTS

CAREER GUIDANCE & PLACEMENT CELL (CG & PC):

Career Guidance & Placement Cell was established in the year 2010 with an objective of providing career guidance, thus leading to placements across different industry verticals across the country.

The CG & PC is structured so well that it has a Placement Officer Mr. N. Sridhar for Non-Engineering streams (UG & PG) and Mr. K.Satyanarayana Reddy, for the Engineering streams duly supported by a team of Faculty Placement Coordinators who extend full support in accomplishing the various objectives designed by the CG & PC.

The CG & PC works in close tandem with the Skill Development Centre in providing comprehensive career solutions to the students. The CG & PC is an instrumental signatory of MoUs with PENA4, NASSCOM and Tally Corporation, to enhance the knowledge levels of the students in their respective domains and some more MoUs are in the offing The CG&PC proactively engages itself in institute-industry interface through guest lectures, work shops and seminars on different facets of Technology and Management.
