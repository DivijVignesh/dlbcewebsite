Electrical and Electronics Engineering is the oldest branch in the field of engineering. It is the largest and fastest growing branch field which deals with the academic study and practical applications of electricity, electronics and electromagnetism. The department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) is made functional with an aim to produce qualified engineers with sound knowledge in electrical engineering and a strong background in electronics. The course is designed in such a way that students can be able to apply their knowledge, skills and the most recent technological developments to fulfil the domestic as well as industrial needs in our society. Trained electrical engineers can be able to design electric circuits and equipment. Besides, they can work on large power plants as well as in small hardware companies which includes designing, manufacturing and operating power plants , industrial machinery, electrical motors, computer chips and ignition systems for automobiles, aircrafts, space crafts and all kinds of engines. They play significant role in planning, designing, implementing and evaluating product, services and systems for fulfilling the socio-economic needs of the people.

The department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering was established in 2010 simultaneously with inception of Dr.Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering. The department offers a four year B.E/B.Tech program. It is offering an excellent academia environment to pursue B.Tech. in EEE. It has highly qualified and experienced faculty who strive hard to enhance the students abilities in both curricular and extra-curricular activities. The department is resourceful in terms of infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities. The department has also acquired specialized research facilities with the funds provided by the management. The department is equipped with laboratories such as Network theory, Control System, Electrical Machines, Power electronics, Power system Protection, power system simulation and electrical workshop. It also has audio and visual facility class rooms with LCD and OHP for effective teaching. Vigourous efforts are made to organize seminars, short-term programs, symposia workshops and refresher courses.

The department has membership in GREEN9 ENSAV energy saving club under which it conducts energy auditing and creates an awareness among the people through 9 mantras to save the electrical energy. The institute has received “Green9 Premier ENSAV Award 2020” in recognition to the activities contributed toward club. The head of the department, faculty members and has received “GREEN9 BEST ADVISOR AWARD 2020”, “GREEN9 SDG AWARD 2020”, and “GREEN9 ENSAVER AWARD” for their relentless services and activities conducted under the club.

Department Vision:

To become a pacesetter in bringing out globally competent electrical and electronics engineers, innovators, researchers, etc., and thereby contribute the value to the knowledge-based economy and society.

Department Mission:
  • To offer good quality Under-Graduate in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

  • To provide state-of-the-art resources that contribute to achieve excellence in teaching-learning, research and development activities.

  • To bridge the gap between industry and academia by framing curricula and syllabi based on industrial and societal needs.

  • To provide suitable forums to enhance the creative talents of students and faculty members.

  • To enable students to develop skills to solve complex technological problems of current times and also provide a framework for promoting collaborative and multidisciplinary activities.

  • To inculcate moral and ethical values among the faculty and students.
