About BS & H

Department of Basic Sciences & Humanities was launched in the year 2010 to function under the umbrella of Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering which was established by the Society for Collegiate Education, Visakhapatnam. State-of-the-art infrastructure is provided for creating Perfect Learning Ambience and a team of Highly Qualified and Committed Teachers strive incessantly to train the budding engineers and technologists.

The Prime Objective of the department is to impart fundamental concepts in Engineering Mathematics, Engineering Chemistry, Engineering Physics, English, Soft Skills, Environmental Studies and Physical Education. Students learn the Basic Principles and Laws in Theoretical Mathematics, Chemistry and Physics in the first year of their study and such knowledge enables them to learn the Practical Applications in Applied Sciences such as Computers, Electricals, Electronics, Communication, Civil etc.

The department also provides adequate facilities for students to acquire knowledge from various sources such as Well Stacked Department Libraries, Fully- Furnished Science Laboratories and Language Laboratories. Alongside these core subjects and domain skills, Soft Skills training is provided to deal with students’ inner Engineering namely enhancing their confidence levels and developing their leadership qualities. Spacious Air-conditioned Seminar Halls are used for conducting Workshops and Symposia. Skill Development Centre serves as one of the wings of the college and it conducts Placement Drives. It immensely helps the students in sharpening their skills for securing the lucrative jobs in the Private and Public Sector Organizations.

The Uninterrupted Internet Facility provides continuous Wi-Fi connectivity to students and faculty members. The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) another important unit in the college, constantly monitors the academic progress of students and the teaching efficiency of the teachers. Proper care is taken to develop student’s overall personality, bringing out the students hidden virtues through activities such as NCC, NSS, Sports, Nature Club, Literary and Cultural Competitions. Some faculty members in the department have been serving as Major in NCC, NSS Program Officer, Physical Director and Cultural Coordinator. The entire college campus is kept under the surveillance of CCTV Camera for making the college a ragging free campus.
